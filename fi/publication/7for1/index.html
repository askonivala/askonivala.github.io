7 for the Price of 1

MRCVE, Johnny Amore (toim.)

Abstrakti

In our case we can talk about 'performance inside the performance' as it makes a critique about the commercialisation of art by doing a work of art. They are asking themselves and the public if performance art can be profitable and they are representing this dilemma in a series of performances. – Irene Pascual
